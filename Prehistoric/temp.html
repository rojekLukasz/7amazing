1.Silbury Hill, Avebury.

Location: Silbury Hill, A4, West Kennet, Marlborough SN8 1QH

The largest artificial mound in Europe, mysterious Silbury Hill compares in height and volume to the roughly contemporary Egyptian pyramids. Probably completed in around 2400 BC, it apparently contains no burial.
